SAMCIS
The School of Accountancy, Management, Computing and Information Studies (SAMCIS) is located at New Site Bakakeng Sur, Baguio City. It is a fusion School of Accountancy and Business Management (SABM) and School of Computing and Information Sciences (SCIS).
